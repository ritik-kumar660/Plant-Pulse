# Plant-Care-App
Plant Pulse is an intelligent plant identification and care management web application designed to support plant enthusiasts and gardeners at all levels.
By leveraging image recognition technology, the platform identifies plant species from uploaded photos and delivers comprehensive care guidelines, including watering schedules, sunlight and soil requirements, and pest management solutions.

The system also features personalized plant tracking, automated watering reminders, and expert recommendations to help users maintain healthy plants. With an intuitive interface and data-driven insights, Plant Pulse simplifies plant care and promotes sustainable gardening practices.
